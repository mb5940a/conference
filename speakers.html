Speakers


Zoe Quinn

Zoe Quinn is a game designer, programmer, artist, and writer. She is known for creating “Depression Quest”, an interactive video game that has players simulate what life is like living with depression.


Jason Santa Maria

Jason Santa Maria is a web designer and product leader in the design industry. He co-founded A Book Apart, was the Executive Design Director of Vox Media, and is currently the Senior Design Director at InVision.


Rebecca Solnit

Rebecca Solnit is an acclaimed writer with notable literary works on feminism, art, history, and the environment. Most recently, she wrote an feminist adaptation of the classic story of Cinderella.


Chappell Ellison

Chappell is a designer, writer, and educator that has worked with the MOMA and the Designer Observer. She received the AIGA Winterhouse Award for her essay on design issues related to Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder.


Shannon Mattern

Shannon is an anthropology professor at the New School in NYC. Her research focuses on media spaces such as spatial epistomology, urban data, media infrastructures, and public design.


Kristy Tillman

Kristy is graphic designer with experience in brand identity, visual storytelling, and digital product development. She is currently Head of Global Experience Design at Slack and based in the Bay Area.


Zeynep Tufekci

Zeynep is a professor and sociologist known for her research on the social implications of emmerging technologies on politics and corporate responsibility. She is also a monthly contributor for the New York Times’ Technology section.


Manoush Zamorodi

Manoush is a podcast host, journalist, and author. She is known for “Note to Self”, a WNYC podcast about human’s relationships with technology and for her recent podcast, “ZigZag”.
