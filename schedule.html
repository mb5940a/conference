Schedule


8-8:30am

Check-in

Check in at the hotel lobby of the Marriott Marquis. There will be signs guiding you through to the main conference room. You will recieve your conference stress ball and stickers upon check-in.


8:30-9:30am

Welcome Address

Welcome Address by Rebecca Solnit on the meaning of bloom; and understanding mental health + emotion in design. A short Q & A will follow the welcome address. Coffee, tea, juice, and pastries provided.


10am-12pm

Meet & Share

Meet other designers/attendees at the conference and showcase your current projects to receive more feedback. Bring a laptop computer to share your work. During this time, you will also be able to network with attending speakers.


12-1:30pm

Catered Lunch from Cava

Lunch provided; Time to meet other attendees and sit at occupation-specific tables: Graphic/Visual Design, Industrial Design, UX / UI / Web Design, & General Design. You may also sit at any table at which you'd like to meet professionals from that design area. Note: If you have any dietary restrictions or allergies, please email the bloom; team as soon as possible.


2-3pm

Keynote Address by Zoe Quinn

Hear from Zoe Quinn on the role mental health has played in her own life, her Depression Quest game, and how she navigated the “Gamergate” controversy in 2014. A short Q & A will follow.

3:30-5:30pm

Small Workshops


3:30-5:30pm

Unlocking Your Mental Health

Share with a small circle of others about your personal, emotional, or mental struggles in design. Led by Chappell Ellison, who will also discuss her essay on design issues related to Obsessive Compulsive Disorder.


3:30-5:30pm

Using Emotion in Design

Learn from professional UX designer, Kristy Tillman, on how to effectively incorporate emotion into design in any industry. A short Q & A will follow.


4:30-5:30pm

Meditation & Yoga

Unwind and relax as you are led in group yoga, followed by meditation. Open to everyone. The purpose of this workshop is to learn about the healthy benefits of excercise and meditation while having fun.


6-7pm

Closing Address By Jason Santa Maria

Hear about Jason’s personal experiences as a design professional affected by mental health and the lessons that he’s learned. A thoughtful Q & A will follow.

7-8:30pm

Dinner at Anthem by Marriott

Enjoy a casual dinner with fellow attendees and conference speakers. Note: If you have any dietary restrictions or allergies, please email the bloom; team as soon as possible.


9-11pm

Evening Drinks & Tech Conversations

Enjoy closing social time with Zeynep Tufekci, Manoush Zamorodi, and Shannon Mattern who have looked at human relationships with technology. A short Q & A will follow.

January 10th, 2019
